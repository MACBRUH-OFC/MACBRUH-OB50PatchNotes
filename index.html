CHARACTER BALANCE (ADJUSTMENTS)

Nairi

Nairi can make your Gloo Wall stronger and heal herself and her teammates, giving her a significant advantage in both long-range and close-range combat. Therefore, we've decided to slightly reduce her survivability for better balance.

Ice Iron: Within 2s → 1.5s after using Gloo Wall, restores 160 durability/s for Gloo Wall and 10 HP/s → 5 HP/s for teammates within 5m. The healing effect cannot be stacked with multiple Gloo Walls.

Kairos

Kairos boosts attacks with a powerful burst of penetration damage, giving them an edge in gunfights. Currently, the burst lasts a full 20 seconds—long enough to turn the tide in teamfights. This time, we've shortened that duration by increasing the EP consumption to balance gameplay.

Defense Breaker: Defense Mode: Recovers 2 EP/s continuously. Breaker Mode: Activates when EP is full and the skill is used for the first time when attacking an enemy with a shield/armor. During this mode, the user consumes 10 EP/s → 20 EP/s. Each hit on a shielded/armored enemy will reduce the armor's Shield Points/Durability by 120% of the damage dealt. When EP reaches 0, return to Defense Mode.

Sonia

Sonia's Nanoshield allows you to perform crucial eliminations and, when paired with Dimitri, enables automatic help-ups after being knocked down. However, this can be frustrating for opponents. To make her counter-eliminations and self-revives feel more balanced, we're reducing the strength of her shield.

Nano Lifeshield: After receiving fatal damage, enters an invulnerable state and cannot move for 0.4s. Then, gains a shield of 50 HP → 1 HP that lasts for 2s. The user is eliminated when the shield disappears. Cooldown: 180s.

Santino

In Battle Royale mode, some players were using Santino to grab weapons from the ground after landing on top of buildings, then quickly return to the rooftop. This tactic allowed them to ambush from unexpected angles, disrupting other players' experience. To address this, we've restricted this ability.

Shape Splitter: Spawn a mannequin with 200 HP that moves automatically for 25 seconds. When the mannequin is within 90m, use the skill again to swap positions with it. You can only swap once every 3 seconds, and a maximum of two times. The player who destroys the mannequin will be marked for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 60s. New mechanism: Cannot teleport if the mannequin is 10m higher than the user.

Laura

Laura's power was meant to help long-range shooters become more accurate, but after several patches and new characters, her skills started to lag. In the next patch, we're increasing her accuracy boost to make Laura even more effective at long-range shooting.

Sharp Shooter: Accuracy increased from 50% → 60% when using scope.

Alvaro

Alvaro's cluster grenades weren't deadly enough, so we increased his damage to make him a stronger bomber.

Splitz Blitz: Damage from explosive weapons increased by 20% and range increased by 10%. Each grenade will split into 3 mini grenades upon explosion, each dealing 20% → 25% of the original grenade's damage.

WEAPON CUSTOMIZATION

Machine Guns: M60 & M249

"New versions of the M60 and M249 now support attachments, increasing upgrade potential and overall power!"

  • M60: Added slots for muzzle and stock
  • M249: Added slots for muzzle and stock

Marksman Rifle: AC80

"The AC80 has been neglected for quite some time. In the new patch, the AC80 will be fully upgraded, making it more competitive among marksman weapons."

AC80 (Buff): Rate of Fire +5%, Damage +20%, Consecutive hit damage bonus -20%

Assault Rifle: AN94

"The AN94 has good damage and a good rate of fire, but its excessive spread limits its performance. As a rarely used weapon, its accuracy needs to be improved to make it more useful."

AN94 (Buff): Accuracy +10%

Pistols: M1917 & FGL-24

"The M1917 performed extremely well in Clash Squad, rivaling many rifles and SMGs. This new patch will slightly nerf the M1917 while still making it a cost-effective option."

M1917 (Nerf): Damage -10%

"As one of the few weapons that can penetrate Gloo Walls and effectively damage enemy armor, the FGL-24 poses a significant threat in combat. We aim to nerf its armor-piercing capabilities while retaining its Gloo Wall penetration capabilities to reduce its overall impact."

FGL24 (Nerf): Damage to Armor - 50%

Shotgun: M590

"With its lightning-fast reload speed, the M590 combines high damage with stable sustained fire. This new patch will slightly slow its reload speed but increase its minimum damage to emphasize its initial strength."

M590 (Adjustment): Minimum Damage +15%, Reload Speed -20%

SMG: Bizon

"As the only SMG with powerful aim assist, the Bizon's large fire spread limits its effectiveness. This new patch will improve the Bizon's accuracy, making its combination of precision and aim assist even more competitive."

Bizon (Buff): Accuracy +10%

Snipers: VSK94 & M82B

"While both can be found on the battlefield, the VSK94 and M82B have very different strengths. The M82B excels in high damage and Gloo Wall penetration, while the VSK94 is often overlooked. Since both have similar drop conditions, the power difference needs to be reduced. This new patch will nerf the M82B and adjust the VSK94."

VSK94 (Adjustment): Removes Aim-Assist, Range +10%

M82B (Nerf): Damage through Gloo Wall -15%
